Research Interests: Machine Learning,  Music DPS, Parallel and Distributed Computing, Computer Music, Music Production

I am an enthusiast of computer sciences, I have special interest in two areas: distributed systems and Machine learning.

I worked in the laboratories of IBM Research and Development Germany in the migration of Data mining alghoritms from netezza platform to DashDB (Cloud) and BigInsights (Big data) Platforms, I developed a system of statistical analysis for the supercomputer "Apolo"​ in the EAFIT University and have worked in the development of distributed systems, geographical analysis and textual analysis for the RiSE group. In the Center of Excellence and appropriation on Big Data and Data Analytics (CAOBA) I have deployed virtual labs for machine learning  with python skalearn, caffe and tensorflow and for statistics with R, also have helped in the management of datasets using ckan and colaborative code development with git.

In my bachelor in computer science I focused in the development of distributed systems, back end, concurrency, OS, databases and the development of algorithms.

I was in the German university TU Ilmenau in the masters degree in computer science where I researched about data mining and machine learning. Now I'm at the EAFIT University  writing a paper on Generative Deep Recurrent Networks applied to sequences using continuous vectorial space representations for symbolic music composition  and the use of GPU for performance optimization 

I am also Musician, composer and producer, and I'm really interested in the use of Artificial Intelligence to generate Art, and the implications that it could have in the development of cognition in the intelligent systems, I am specially interested in future research about machine learning in audio signals for music composition, production and audio effects.

I'm always happy to work with new technologies, it fascinates me to learn new things
