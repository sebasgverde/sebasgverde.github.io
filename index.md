Research Interests: Machine Learning, Music DSP, Parallel and Distributed Computing, Computer Music

I am an enthusiast of computer sciences with a particular interest in Machine learning and distributed systems. In my bachelor in computer science, I focused on the development of distributed systems (back end, concurrency, OS, databases and the development of algorithms). I hold an M.Sc degree in Engineering (machine learning) from EAFIT University with studies in the German university TU Ilmenau where I approved the emphasis line in machine learning of the M.Sc in computer science and researched about [evolutionary heuristics](https://sebasgverde.github.io/evolutiveSudoku/).

I am also Musician, composer and producer. I play the guitar since 15 years ago (plus other eight instruments since fewer years) and have composed many [songs](https://sebasgverde.github.io/music/) which you can listen to on my [youtube channel](https://exit.sc/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.youtube.com%2Fchannel%2FUCI7GUA0TNB0OKZSDjSv-itg), [facebook page](https://exit.sc/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.facebook.com%2Fsebasmusica%2F) or [SoundCloud](https://soundcloud.com/sebastian-garcia-valencia). I am really interested in the use of Artificial Intelligence to generate Art and the implications that it could have in the development of cognition in the intelligent systems, thus I usually try to merge these disciplines in my [projects](https://sebasgverde.github.io/software/) and [posts](https://sebasgverde.github.io/blogs/). For my master thesis, I have a [paper](https://www.slideshare.net/slideshow/embed_code/key/3RlggZsYZkl8Eh) in publishing process on "Generative Deep Recurrent Networks applied to sequences using continuous vectorial space representations for symbolic music composition". I am especially interested in future research about [machine learning in audio signals](https://sebasgverde.github.io/basicMLMusicDSP/) for music composition, production and audio effects.

I have worked in the laboratories of IBM R&D (Germany) in the migration of Data mining algorithms to DashDB (Cloud) and BigInsights (Big data), the HPC centre ["Apolo"](http://www.eafit.edu.co/centros/apolo/Paginas/supercomputing.aspx),the [RiSE Group](http://rise-group.org/), where I worked in distributed systems, NLP and Convolutional Neural Networks for object classification.In the Center of Excellence and Appropriation on Big Data and Data Analytics ([CAOBA](http://alianzacaoba.co/que-es-caoba/)) I deployed virtual labs for machine learning and worked in the visualization of networks for bank data ([Bancolombia](https://www.grupobancolombia.com/wps/portal/personas)) and analytics of social data for marketing ([Nutresa](https://www.gruponutresa.com/)).

Right know I am working in Luxembourg as Research And Development Engineer in ([AIVA Technologies](https://www.aiva.ai/about)), the first AI recognised as a compouser by an authors’ right society, where I adapt and reapply proven methodologies from other domains, such as natural language processing and Computer vision as well as develop novel solutions to unsolved problems using machine learning. 

I'm always happy to work with new technologies, learn new things and participate in interesting projects, feel free to contact me if you have one.
