Research Interests: Machine Learning, Music DPS, Parallel and Distributed Computing, Computer Music

I am an enthusiast of computer sciences with a particular interest in Machine learning and distributed systems. In my bachelor in computer science, I focused in the development of distributed systems, back end, concurrency, OS, databases and the development of algorithms. I am finishing my M.Sc in Engineering (machine learning) in the EAFIT University with studies in the German university TU Ilmenau where I approved the emphasis line in machine learning of the M.Sc in computer science and researched about [evolutionary heuristics](https://sebasgverde.github.io/evolutiveSudoku/).

I am also Musician, composer and producer. I play the guitar since 15 years ago (plus other eight instruments since fewer years) and have composed many [songs](https://sebasgverde.github.io/music/) which you can listen to on my [youtube channel](https://exit.sc/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.youtube.com%2Fchannel%2FUCI7GUA0TNB0OKZSDjSv-itg), [facebook page](https://exit.sc/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.facebook.com%2Fsebasmusica%2F) or [SoundCloud](https://soundcloud.com/sebastian-garcia-valencia). I am really interested in the use of Artificial Intelligence to generate Art and the implications that it could have in the development of cognition in the intelligent systems, thus I usually try to merge these disciplines in my [projects](https://sebasgverde.github.io/software/) and [posts](https://sebasgverde.github.io/blogs/). For my master thesis, I have a [paper](https://www.slideshare.net/slideshow/embed_code/key/3RlggZsYZkl8Eh) in publishing process on "Generative Deep Recurrent Networks applied to sequences using continuous vectorial space representations for symbolic music composition". I am especially interested in future research about [machine learning in audio signals](https://sebasgverde.github.io/basicMLMusicDSP/) for music composition, production and audio effects.

I have worked in the laboratories of IBM R&D (Germany) in the migration of Data mining algorithms to DashDB (Cloud) and BigInsights (Big data), the HPC centre ["Apolo"](http://www.eafit.edu.co/centros/apolo/Paginas/supercomputing.aspx) and the [RiSE Group](http://rise-group.org/) , where I worked in the development of distributed systems, geographical analysis, NLP and researched about Convolutional Neural Networks for object classification in urban context. Right now I am consultant in the Center of Excellence and Appropriation on Big Data and Data Analytics ([CAOBA](http://alianzacaoba.co/que-es-caoba/)) where I have deployed virtual labs for machine learning and have worked in the visualization of networks for bank data ([Bancolombia](https://www.grupobancolombia.com/wps/portal/personas)) and analytics of social data for marketing ([Nutresa](https://www.gruponutresa.com/)).

I'm always happy to work with new technologies, learn new things and participate in interesting projects, feel free to contact me if you have one.
