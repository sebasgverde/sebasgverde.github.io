Research Interests: Machine Learning, Music DSP, Parallel and Distributed Computing, Computer Music, Logic, Formal Systems, among others.

In my bachelor in computer science, I focused on the development of distributed systems (back end, concurrency, OS, databases and the development of algorithms). I hold an M.Sc degree in Engineering (machine learning) from EAFIT University with studies in the TU Ilmenau (Germany) where I approved the emphasis line in machine learning of the M.Sc in computer science and researched about [evolutionary heuristics](https://sebasgverde.github.io/evolutiveSudoku/).

For my master thesis, I have a [paper](https://www.slideshare.net/slideshow/embed_code/key/3RlggZsYZkl8Eh) in publishing process on "Generative Deep Recurrent Networks applied to symbolic music composition". I am especially interested in future research about [machine learning in audio signals](https://sebasgverde.github.io/basicMLMusicDSP/) for music composition, production and audio effects.

I have worked in the laboratories of IBM R&D (Germany), the HPC centre ["Apolo"](http://www.eafit.edu.co/centros/apolo/Paginas/supercomputing.aspx), the [RiSE Group](http://rise-group.org/) and the Center of Excellence and Appropriation on Big Data and Data Analytics ([CAOBA](http://alianzacaoba.co/que-es-caoba/)).

Right know I am working in Luxembourg as Research And Development Engineer in ([AIVA Technologies](https://www.aiva.ai/about)), the first AI recognised as a compouser by an authors’ right society, where I adapt and reapply proven methodologies from other domains, such as natural language processing and Computer vision as well as develop novel solutions to unsolved problems using machine learning.
